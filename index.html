A Jill of All Trades

My work history starts as a young teenager working at the local dirt racetrack with my dad and younger brother. There I learned basic working skills and even a little about cars, engines and offensive driving.

In my more recent years, I moved into luxury retail and body modification. This is the first job I've ever had where I needed better office tools to help develop my staff and business procedures. This need jump started my curiosity about code.
